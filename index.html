Process

Structured, transparent and outcome-focused.

Whether we are working with a hedge fund, a private equity firm or a company raise, the rhythm stays the same: understand, design, distribute and close.

Step one

Triage call and mandate definition

We start with a short call to hear your story in your own words. We cover current assets or target raise, investor base, fee structure and what you want to achieve over the next six to twelve months. We also discuss what you have already tried and why you are exploring alternatives now.

Step two

Investor universe and data build

We define the investor groups that make the most sense for your mandate – for example sovereign wealth funds in the Gulf, multi-family offices in Europe or pensions in North America. We then build a curated universe using our Rolodex, consolidated databases and in-house tools.

Step three

Campaign launch and relationship cultivation

Our team deploys high-volume yet carefully targeted campaigns using private email infrastructure and direct calling. Messaging evolves as data comes in. We pre-qualify interest, coordinate meetings and ensure your time is focused on high-signal conversations only.

Step four

Due diligence, negotiation and closing support

For interested investors we prepare background notes, tailor meetings, assist with negotiations and support due diligence. You receive regular reporting and clear decision points at each stage.

What you can expect from the first two calls
  • Call one: discovery, discussion of investor groups and an honest view on whether we are a fit.
  • Internal work: initial investor mapping, a draft approach and fee outline.
  • Call two: review of scope, timelines, investor universe and next steps.
  • If aligned: final engagement agreement and onboarding with the backend team.

At each step we aim for clarity. Either it makes sense to proceed together, or it does not. Both outcomes are acceptable; the important thing is that the decision is informed.

Fees

Aligned economics, clearly explained.

Our model combines an upfront engagement fee with a success-based fee. We only succeed fully when your raise or transaction does.

Engagement fees
US$5,000 – US$25,000+
Typically covering six to twelve months of work.

Engagement fees reflect the depth and intensity of the mandate and usually cover:

  • A dedicated team lead with investment banking or venture capital experience.
  • An analyst pod to build and maintain investor or deal lists.
  • System build-out, including servers, domains and compliance requirements.
  • Ongoing optimisation of outreach scripts and regular reporting.

The specific fee is proposed after the triage call once your objectives, timelines and existing efforts are fully understood.

Success-based fees
0.5% – 4%
On capital raised or transaction value.

Back-end economics are structured to align our incentives with yours:

  • Fund distribution: generally between 0.5 and 1 per cent on capital raised.
  • Private equity and transaction work: usually 0.5 to 2.5 per cent on deal value.
  • Direct equity raises: typically between 4 and 6 per cent, with tiers for larger rounds.

All terms are set out clearly in a draft engagement agreement and refined before onboarding.
